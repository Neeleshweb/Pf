Why it’s not a forum
Anonymous In Public. Credible Underneath.

DecisionRoom is a private room for pressure-testing the hard product calls — the ones with no clean answer — before you commit to them. You post a real decision under an alias, so the room reacts to the substance of the call, not your company or your title.

And underneath every alias sits visible, earned credibility — the structured second opinion Reddit, Slack, or a LinkedIn post can’t give you, because here you can actually see whose judgment to trust.

🛡️ Anonymous by default

Posts show an alias and PM context only — e.g. “Senior PM · B2B SaaS · 7y.” Your name and company are never shown unless you choose to, per case.

✅ Credible reviewers

Reviewers carry verified PM context, and some carry a judgment score earned in GutCheck — demonstrated product judgment, not just a job title.

🧱 Structure over noise

Every response needs a recommendation, reasoning, risks, and a next step. No “+1”, no vague takes, no sponsored advice.

🔒 Safe by design

A confidentiality check before every post, and reporting on anything unsafe. Built for real decisions, handled with care.

Two tools, one PM
GutCheck or DecisionRoom?

They solve different problems — one builds the instinct over many reps, the other helps you with the one real call in front of you. Most PMs use both.

🎯 GutCheck
Build the instinct
Best forAspiring PMs prepping for interviews — and any PM who wants to sharpen judgment with quick daily reps.
Use it whenYou want fast, low-stakes practice: commit your call on a fresh dilemma before you see what the crowd did.
You walk away withA calibration score, a judgment level, and a track record you can actually point to.
vs
🧭 DecisionRoom
Pressure-test the real call
Best forWorking PMs facing a real, high-stakes decision right now — with aspiring PMs learning as reviewers.
Use it whenThe answer isn’t obvious, the stakes are real, and smart people on your team disagree.
You walk away withStructured feedback from credible PMs, the tradeoffs compared, and a defensible next move.
The short version: GutCheck sharpens how you think across many small reps. DecisionRoom helps you make the one big decision in front of you — and turns it into a playbook for the next PM.
