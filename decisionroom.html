Two tools, one PM
GutCheck or DecisionRoom?

They solve different problems — one builds the instinct over many reps, the other helps you with the one real call in front of you. Most PMs use both.

🎯 GutCheck
Build the instinct
Best forAspiring PMs prepping for interviews — and any PM who wants to sharpen judgment with quick daily reps.
Use it whenYou want fast, low-stakes practice: commit your call on a fresh dilemma before you see what the crowd did.
You walk away withA calibration score, a judgment level, and a track record you can actually point to.
vs
🧭 DecisionRoom
Pressure-test the real call
Best forWorking PMs facing a real, high-stakes decision right now — with aspiring PMs learning as reviewers.
Use it whenThe answer isn’t obvious, the stakes are real, and smart people on your team disagree.
You walk away withStructured feedback from credible PMs, the tradeoffs compared, and a defensible next move.
The short version: GutCheck sharpens how you think across many small reps. DecisionRoom helps you make the one big decision in front of you — and turns it into a playbook for the next PM.
